Our Services

3PS understands that this is a new step for the industry and many artists will be at different levels of understanding and proficiency when it comes to managing cryptocurrency (digital currency that can be used to buy goods and services) and blockchains (cumulative records that are used to record transactions across many computers so that no transaction cannot be altered retroactively, allowing participants to verify and audit transactions). 3PS is prepared to help with any custom service requested by our clients.

A&R
3PS Specializes in talent scouting and the development of recording artists. Our job is to find you the best studios, producers, and collaborations that will help you achieve your creative endeavors. 


Digital PR
3PS will promote and display your hard copy and/or NFT's of choice along with any promotional data provided through the 3PS website, social media, our virtual venue and any advertising companies we collaborate with.


NFT Consulting
3PS will help teach how to set up a digital wallet where you will receive your sale profits and royalties, how to exchange cryptocurrency, how to “mint” (validate ownership in a blockchain) your own NFT's, and how to build your own marketplace. 


Management
3PS will help organize , structure and help facilitate your collections, book virtual and RL gigs, orchestrate your releases and help pursue your long-term career goals.


3D/AR Design & Animation
For artists who would like to give their digital art or hard copy a little pizazz with some animation, our 3D visual artist can help bring your work to life!


Virtual Reality Avatar Video Shoot- Coming soon!
Want to have some fun and take things to a more creative level? The 3PS design team can design you a custom avatar and provide a virtual live or recorded session for an AR/VR video. Studio is located in New York.
