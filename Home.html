What we are

Third Planet Studio is a non traditional virtual music and art group that ensures each of the artists have 100% ownership of their work and maintain full creative freedom to express themselves without limitations.

Third Planet Studio promotes artists/musicians/performers by displaying their work and merchandise to the public through the medium of multiple social platforms, multiple virtual stages in the metaverse, the 3PS social media and website, and the many 3PS partnership communities.

Our ultimate goal is to help artists make and market not only their NFT's, but also help grow their following and provide them the resources needed to thrive in the Web3 community.

What we do

At 3PS, we will help artists make their own rules and build NFT Smart contracts to their personal specifications. Our main focus is to make sure that the artists and performers do not get scammed in the manner of the "old ways" of the proverbial “industry.”

Unlike a record label or gallery, we want the artists to have full ownership of their work and retain the dignity associated with their life’s passion.
