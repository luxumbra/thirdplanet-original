Who is gulches?

Gulches is a Bellarmine University Music Technology BM graduate with six years experience mixing, mastering, recording and editing music. Gulches focuses on artist relations and making sure their music sounds exactly as they imagined it, as well as offering creative development, engineering and production for any project!

What's in the name?

Gulches comes from his strong ties to the thoroughbred horse industry that his family raised him in. In 1995, the year he was born, a champion racehorse Thunder Gulch won the Kentucky Derby. The plurality of Gulches also signifies his many lanes of work, in music stylings as well as engineering and recording.
