Cooper Guitar

Teacher / Guitarist / Music Producer

Who am I?


Hi! My name is Alon Cooper, and my mission is to create as many smiles and good feelings as possible, using the songs that I write on my journeys.

🌎 I am originally from Israel, and over the last 8 years, (After finishing my 5 years army service in Israel as a Paratrooper officer) I have been mostly traveling the world with my guitars, collecting songs and experiences, and performing with my band/solo at 100's of different venues. My "Northern star" is being "the next Jack Johnson" dare I say, with music that has all the exotic influences I've gathered while living abroad in Australia, the USA, Europe, and more.

🤓 I've earned my university degree in education and business somewhere along those years and between the travels.

🎹 To make a living, in the last 8 years I have also been running my business of online courses, teaching people how to develop their own style on the guitar, piano, and harmonica. Over 100K students have joined so far.

❤️ Have a BEAUTIFUL day. Cooper
