Christopher Williams

Christopher Williams – better known as Damas Music, is a Reggae musician born and raised in Kingtson, Jamaica. The artist has a plentiful portfolio and is also renown as an audio engineer working with wide cross-spectrum of top vocalist, musicians and producers across the reggae / dancehall diaspora. Damas – released his debut project titled the “Natty Take Ova” - an EP in late 2015 since then the artist has completed multiple tours, released numerous singles and have been working tirelessly to remain relevant in this forever changing music industry. The artist is set to release his follow up project titled “The Jonestown Story” later in 2021 which will take you on not only a musical passage but also a visual and virtual journey of the true life experience in Jamaica. His mission is to bring back positive music to the youths of the struggling communities while preserving the roots and recognition of reggae.
