Gumbi
Ortiz

Born and raised in the South Bronx, Gumbi is a master conga percussionist makes up his own genre by blending his Puerto Rican/Cuban roots with jazz, fusion, Caribbean and funk.  At age 7, Gumbi learned to play the piano and saxophone and was playing in his first band in New York City at age 11 alongside the "greats" of the time. He then relocated to south Florida with his family and is now known around the world for his music and his extensive knowledge of percussion.

About Gumbi

 Gumbi leads many musical groups including Florida based “New” Groove City”,  bringing back love for the fusion genre, he started “Elecitrik Rendezvous” and has been the drum master for guitar legend, Al DI Meola since the mid 80's with non stop tours around the world.   When he takes the stage, he not only takes his music to its highest level but he brings the audience along for an exciting musical journey and makes sure there is a smile on every face in the crowd!

Experience

Gumbi has Recorded, performed, and/or Produced, some of today’s most diverse and acclaimed  musicians on the Planet  including : Phillippe Saisse, Jeff Berlin, Steve Vai, Nat Adderley, Joel Taylor, Cyril Neville, Irvin Mayfield, Bill Summers, Armand Sabal-Lecco, Senri Kawaguchi, Chick Corea, Gato Barbieri, Rick Ross, Young Jeezy, The J.U.S.T.I.C.E League,Jeff Lorber, Dave Weckl, Eric Marienthal, Brandon Field ,Jay Beckenstein, Barry Miles, Joel Rosenblatt ,The Black Honkeys, Nick Brignola, Jim Pepper, Ted Shumate, Fred Johnson, Irene Cara, kamau kenyatta, Walter Booker, Jimmy Cobb, Rachel Z, Lenny White, kei Akagi,Leonid Agutin, Roman Miroshnichenko, Rick Braun, The Allen Carman Project, Tom Brechtlein, Luis Alicea, New Groove City, The Electrik Rendezvous, Marc Quiñones and Sammy Figueroa, to name but a few.

New Groove City

Gumbi is also a teacher, session player and leader of Florida-based “New Groove City”. The band is influenced from music around the world and founded on the rhythms of Afro Cuban, jazz, rumba American R & B, funk while taking you on an adventure of hardcore timba and real Cuban salsa music.  
The band has released two successful albums, La Cura (2000) and Soy De Aqui (2005) and has toured throughout Florida and the Southeast.
Featuring top Latin jazz artists, you can guarantee a high energy show.
