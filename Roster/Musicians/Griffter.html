Music Producer / Songwriter / Metaverse DJ

Who is he?

For Griffter, the essence of music can be felt in its purest form on
the dance floor. Coincidentally, it’s also what drives him to create
his brand of cerebral, hypnotic, and groove-heavy house. 

Griffter’s sound is often described as the perfect blend of funky
and melodic, with a focus on pulsing beats and driving basslines
that go down in both beach party and club settings. As a
classically trained musician proficient in violin and piano, Griffter
also incorporates his breadth of musical knowledge to his
productions, with intricately laced chords, melodies and pads
being a defining feature of his tracks.

His latest single, “I Go Like This” is making it’s rounds in the
streaming world, while his latest project, “The Games We’ve
Played” is making its way on OpenSea. TGWP is a dedication to
the games Griffter played while growing up, it an ode to the music
and the stories that fascinated him as a boy.

Griffter’s got another project coming out soon as a music NFT and
he plans to make it assessable to everyone!
