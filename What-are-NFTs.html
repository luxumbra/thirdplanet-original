What are NFT's? 

NFT's are Non- Fungible tokens. They are a type of digital asset that is a signature of ownership. Once a piece of media goes through the minting process (digital stamp), ownership of NFT's location are recorded on a blockchain where they cannot be replicated.

A digital asset can represent real-world objects (Land or a home) or intellectual property, copyrights or patents (music, artwork, video) Most anything of value can be traded and tracked on a blockchain.

The biggest benefit of NFT's when it comes to artists and musicians is that the creators can be paid their royalties instantaneously.

Each NFT is unique
Buyers can collect, sell or trade NFTs
The artists can receive a chosen royalty % each time the NFT is resold. NFTS are also integrated with metaverses, video games and digital worlds. 

What is Crypto Currency? 

Cryptocurrencies are a form of digital or virtual currency that run on a technology called Blockchain. There are many different cryptocurrencies out there like Bitcoin, Ethereum, Litecoin and the meme coin Doge. Crypto currency doesn't rely on central banks or third parties to verify transactions but rather uses cryptography to verify transactions on a public ledger called a blockchain. 

What is a blockchain? 

Blockchain is decentralized technology that allows people to send and receive cryptocurrencies on a community based platform which is near impossible to hack and is protected by complex encryption algorythms. Each time a new transaction takes place on a blockchain, a new block is formed and linked together keeping a permanent record of the history and finances of that transaction.

What are marketplaces? 

A marketplace is where you can find digital gaming items, digital art, music, and other digital assets backed by a blockchain like Ethereum, Binance Smart Chain and Matic (Polygon) to name a few. Depending on the style of NFT's for sale, there are many different marketplaces that cater to more specific items.
Opensea, Rarible, Nifty Gateway and many more are examples of some of the current top marketplaces! 

Why Mint NFT's? 

To get paid fairly and instantaneously.
Artists can program a traceable, transparent, and irreversible Smart contract into their NFT's.
Artists will continue to get paid directly by setting a percentage of sale profits specific to that minted NFT for each time their work is resold.
The creator can set limits on the number of NFT's in existence.
Artists can make each asset unique within its metadata making it different from other assets of that type making those specific NFT's more valuable and rarer.
NFT's asset details are verifiable to the collectors.
To help artist gain a new and upcoming audience and following.
For artists to have a more direct connection with their clients or
Fanbase. 

Virtual Worlds

There are many virtual worlds being developed as we speak. Each has their own unique theme and design. These are not only continuously growing into a fully functioning city marketplace, but they also have full compatibility for AR/VR devices. Some of the current worlds are Decentraland, Cryptovoxels, The Sandbox, and Somnium Space, while many more are in the making! Decentraland is one of the top recognized virtual worlds. Typically, a virtual world gives the users the ability to purchase land and build freely using available tools or importing your own 3D structure. You control your own avatar and can navigate through the world and explore many different creations from amusement parks, casinos, art galleries, interactive games, social venues, and music venues like the 3PS virtual project.
